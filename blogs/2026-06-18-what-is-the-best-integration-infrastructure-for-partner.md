The article examines three approaches to integration infrastructure for partner ecosystems: in-house builds, traditional no-code platforms, and embedded iPaaS solutions. It argues that a code-first embedded iPaaS offers the best balance of developer control and managed infrastructure for scaling customer-facing integrations.
